Channel jamming mitigation explorer

Interactive companion to the local resource conservation proposal (BOLT PR #1280): a channel's incoming resources are split into general / congestion / protected buckets, and each outgoing peer is limited to a slice of the general bucket. Adjust the parameters to see the per-bucket restrictions and the share of observed mainnet edges that can carry a single HTLC of a given dollar size.
